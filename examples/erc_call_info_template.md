## Evaluation Criteria
- **Excellence:** Novelty, high-risk/high-gain, ground-breaking potential.
- **Impact:** Scientific leverage, interdisciplinarity, potential to open new fields.
- **Feasibility:** Credible methodology, environment, resources.

- Panel keywords: `Cosmology`, `Large-scale structure`, `AI for astrophysics`.
